Ayurveda

->

Three Faces of Beauty

According to the science of Ayurveda, Doshas are the inherent intelligence of the body and define the innermost nature of every individual. They orchestrate all the functions of the body, shaping how we look, how our metabolic processes function, how we think and feel, and how we respond to external stimuli.

The 3 Doshas are: Vata, Pitta and Kapha. Each individual constitutes a balance of these 3 energies, which create unique physical, mental and emotional characteristics. By determining which Dosha is most dominant in your constitution, you will be able to decode your inherent essence and match it to skincare, haircare or fragrance that is uniquely yours.
